# Print Troubleshooting:

<Tabs items={['Bed Adhesion', 'Extrusion Issues', 'Mechanical Issues', 'Quality Issues']}>

<Tabs.Tab key="Bed Adhesion">
<>
- Ensure proper bed leveling.
- Use adhesives like glue stick or hairspray for better adhesion.
- Clean the bed surface regularly to remove debris or residue.
- Adjust the nozzle height to ensure the first layer adheres properly.
</>
</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Extrusion Issues">
<>
- Check for clogs in the nozzle.
- Ensure the filament is feeding properly.
- Adjust retraction settings to prevent stringing.
- Verify the extruder motor is functioning correctly.
</>
</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Mechanical Issues">
<>
- Inspect belts and pulleys for wear or looseness.
- Ensure the frame is rigid and properly assembled.
- Lubricate moving parts to reduce friction.
- Check for proper alignment of axes.
</>
</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Quality Issues">
<>
- Adjust print speed for better layer adhesion.
- Optimize cooling fan settings for specific materials.
- Verify slicer settings for layer height and infill.
- Ensure the printer is on a stable surface to reduce vibrations.
</>
</Tabs.Tab>

</Tabs>
