## 3D Modeling Repositories 

Repositories are community-supported hosts containing files typically associated with 3D printing, such as .stl, .3mf, and some STEP files.

<Tabs.Tab key="Maker World">
Hosted by Bambu Labs
- Notable: Contains ready-to-print .3mf files & g-code for their printers. 
- Maker Supplies: Ready-made electronics and motors for several projects.
- Contests: Community modeling contests in different areas such as Hiking and City of the Future.
- Maker Lab: Several generative modeling tools.
- Model types: Free
