## Engineering & 3D Model Resources

<Tabs items={[
  'McMaster-Carr',
  'GrabCAD',
  'Smithsonian 3D Library'
]}>

<Tabs.Tab key="McMaster-Carr">
[**McMaster-Carr**](https://www.mcmaster.com/)  
A massive catalog of industrial parts and hardware. McMaster-Carr is widely used by engineers and designers because it provides downloadable CAD files for nearly every product they sell—ready to drop into your design.

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="GrabCAD">
[**GrabCAD**](https://grabcad.com/library)  
A free online library with millions of 3D models shared by engineers and hobbyists. GrabCAD offers everything from mechanical assemblies to electronic enclosures, all downloadable in various formats like STL, STEP, and SolidWorks.

</Tabs.Tab>

<Tabs.Tab key="Smithsonian 3D Library">
[**Smithsonian 3D Library**](https://3d.si.edu/)  
Explore high-resolution 3D scans of artifacts, fossils, and historical objects from the Smithsonian’s vast collection. These files are perfect for educational use, VR/AR projects, or simply exploring museum pieces interactively.

</Tabs.Tab>

</Tabs>
